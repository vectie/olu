# AI Features Guide

Boost your modeling efficiency using built-in Generative AI.

## 1. AI Assistant
Click **"AI Assistant"** in the top right.
- **Natural Language Modeling**: You can enter "Create a leg structure for a quadruped robot".
- **Batch Modifications**: Try entering "Change the color of all left legs to red".
- **Hardware Suggestions**: Enter "Recommend a suitable motor for this knee joint".

## 2. AI Inspector
Click the **"AI Inspector"** button to perform quality audits.
- **Scoring System**: Each check item is scored 0-10.
- **Evaluation Dimensions**:
    - **Physical Plausibility**: Is the mass positive? Does the inertia matrix satisfy the triangle inequality?
    - **Link Frames**: Are the joint axes unified?
    - **Naming**: Are there any duplicate names?
- **Export Report**: Supports exporting inspection results as a PDF document.
